The Best Movies According to Vedangi

My top 3 movies of all-time

Lucy

Amazing sci-fi movie that shows what we could possibly achieve if we unlock 100% of our brain.

Baby Driver

Movie about a great driver who's forced to work for crime.

The Call

Korean psychological thriller where a serial killer puts another woman's present and past lives on the line, and those lives are connected by a phone in the same home but 20 years apart.
